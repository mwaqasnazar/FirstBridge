SMS Messaging Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

First Bridge Dispatch Services offers optional SMS messaging to keep clients informed about dispatch updates, load opportunities, and service communications. This policy explains how our SMS program works.

How to Opt In

You may opt in to receive SMS messages from First Bridge Dispatch Services by:

  • Checking the SMS consent box on our contact or signup form
  • Verbally agreeing with a dispatcher during onboarding
  • Texting START to our business number

Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of receiving dispatch services.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on dispatch activity and service needs. You may receive messages about load updates, rate confirmations, document requests, and service communications. We will not send promotional spam.

How to Opt Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. You will receive one confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent. To re-enroll, text START.

Help & Supported Keywords

The following keywords are supported at any time:

  • STOP, END, CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, QUIT — Immediately opt out and stop all SMS messages
  • HELP — Receive assistance information and a link to contact us

When you reply HELP, you will receive a response with our support email: info@firstbridge.us. You may also contact us directly at that address for any questions about your SMS subscription.

Costs

First Bridge Dispatch Services does not charge for SMS messages. However, message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply. Contact your carrier for details about your plan.

Privacy & Data Sharing

Your phone number and mobile opt-in consent are never sold, shared, or transferred to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile opt-in data is used solely to deliver the specific SMS communications you have consented to receive from First Bridge Dispatch Services.

We do not purchase, sell, or exchange mobile opt-in lists. Each individual's consent applies only to communications from First Bridge Dispatch Services. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
